About CodeSprint 7.0

Innovate Future, Today!

CodeSprint 7.0 is the 7th iteration of a hackathon series which is a flagship event done by the IEEE Student Branch of IIT in collaboration with the Women in engineering affinity group and the IEEE computer society, that aims to find Young Entrepreneurs who can contribute to the global supply chain with Innovative Solutions. CodeSprint 7.0 is aimed at nurturing and guiding the students from an ideation stage through to the implementation stage of a Tech Based Start-up Business. The lack of ideas reaching the implementation stage and execution is the primal reason for this new initiative.

Previous CodeSprints

CodeSprint 1.0

CodeSprint 1.0 was the first Inter-University Hackathon initiated by IEEE Student Branch of IIT and the Women In Engineering Affinity Group of IIT. It was held in 2015 and attracted teams from different universities in Sri Lanka, making it a popular event. A team from General Sir John Kotelawala Defence University emerged as the winner.

CodeSprint 2.0

With the success of CodeSprint 1.0, the Student Branch continued the excitement with CodeSprint 2.0 for the second consecutive time. Finals were held at the Dialog Head Office Auditorium where 15 out of 81 registered teams competed. Team Light from the University of Moratuwa emerged winners and champions of the event.

CodeSprint 4.0

IEEE Student Branch announced CodeSprint 4.0 in 2019, building on the success of previous years. It provided an opportunity for undergraduates to launch their innovative ideas, and was powered by Dialog Ideamart. The competition had university teams presenting their startup ideas to judges and potential investors, with 12 teams exhibiting their products at the Grand Finale.

CodeSprint 5.0

CodeSprint 5.0 was held for the fifth consecutive time in 2020. Unlike the previous years, CodeSprint 5.0 was Sri Lanka's first-ever Battle of Innovation encompassing Data Science under the topic "bringing together the worlds of Data Science and Sustainable Development to bring in new dimensions of innovation".

CodeSprint 6.0

CodeSprint 6.0 was a hybrid event in 2022, due to the Covid-19 pandemic. The main focus of this iteration was to educate and motivate young individuals to pitch their ideas successfully and effectively. Event Phases

From Idea to Product

CodeSprint 7.0 consist of 4 competitive phases and the Grand Finale. The 4 phases includes the IdeaSprint, DesignSprint, BuildSprint, SprintPitch. The workshops are organized for each phase of the competition and we encourage the participants to join them and get the best benefit out of the event Each and every team that register the CodeSprint 7.0 gets the opportunity to compete in all 4 phases and there would be no elimination of teams. Throughout the competition teams will be working on ideating, implementing and marketing the software project ideas brought forward by them. Points will be awarded for each team by the end of every phase and total points will be taken into consideration when finalizing the top 5 teams for the Grand Finale. The top 10 teams who score the highest marks in the DesignSprint phase will get the opportunity to compete head-to-head with each other in Design IX which is a 24 hour Designathon.

Phase

IdeaSprint

Type

Ideathon

Duration

3 weeks

Weight

25%

During the IdeaSprint, participants will attend workshops, receive guidance from industry experts, and develop business canvas models, innovative ideas, and understanding of the startup ecosystem. They will be required to submit a business plan, pitch video, and showcase their creativity, entrepreneurial skills, and network with like-minded individuals.

Phase

DesignSprint

Type

Designathon

Duration

2 weeks

Weight

15%

During the DesignSprint, participants take their innovative ideas from the IdeaSprint and turn them into low and high-fidelity designs by attending workshops on UI/UX and software. They will submit designs and a design brief to be judged and rated. The top 10 teams advance to Design IX, allowing participants to showcase their design and user experience skills, and receive feedback on their designs.

Phase

BuildSprint

Type

Buildathon

Duration

4 weeks

Weight

40%

During the BuildSprint, participants take designs from DesignSprint and build a Minimum Viable Software Product using guidance, mentorship and workshops on modern technologies. They will submit a technical report to be judged on their development process and technologies used. The top teams will be awarded marks out of 40. The BuildSprint allows participants to demonstrate their software development skills, validate their solution and receive feedback on their software product.

Phase

SprintPitch

Type

Business Pitch

Duration

3 weeks

Weight

20%

During the SprintPitch, the final phase of CodeSprint 7.0, participants present their software product as a marketing pitch in front of judges. They attend workshops on value proposition, target market, and digital marketing. The panel evaluates their pitches based on creativity, feasibility, scalability, market potential, and other criteria. It allows participants to showcase their business and entrepreneurial skills, and potentially attract investment and partnership opportunities. The grand prize and recognition in the industry is on the line as the grand finale of CodeSprint.
